# FUTURE_DS_01
Task 1: Sales Data Analysis

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales data to identify trends, patterns, and key business insights. The goal is to understand how sales vary over time and determine top-performing product categories and sub-categories to support better business decision-making.

Objectives

- Analyze monthly sales trends
- Identify patterns and fluctuations in sales
- Determine top-performing categories and sub-categories
- Extract meaningful business insights

Tools & Technologies

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Google Colab (Jupyter Notebook environment)

Dataset

- Sales dataset containing order details, product categories, sub-categories, and sales values

Analysis Performed

- Data loading and preprocessing
- Conversion of date columns to proper format
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Category-wise sales analysis
- Sub-category performance analysis
- Data visualization using graphs

Key Insights

- Sales show an overall increasing trend over time, indicating business growth
- Sales fluctuate significantly, with noticeable drops around mid-year months
- Technology is the highest revenue-generating category
- Sub-categories like Phones, Chairs, and Storage contribute significantly to total sales

Recommendations

- Focus on boosting sales during low-performing mid-year periods through promotions
- Invest more in high-performing categories like Technology
- Promote top-performing sub-categories such as Phones and Chairs
- Analyze seasonal patterns to improve sales planning and inventory management

Conclusion

The analysis highlights important sales trends and product performance patterns. While the business shows growth, there are fluctuations that indicate seasonal effects. By focusing on high-performing products and improving weaker periods, overall sales performance can be enhanced.
